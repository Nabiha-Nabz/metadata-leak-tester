About the Simulated Metadata Leak Tester

Understanding the invisible risks in your digital files

Our Mission

In today's digital world, we share files constantly - photos, documents, and more. But these files often contain hidden metadata that can reveal more than we intend. Our tool helps you understand what metadata is and what risks it might pose to your privacy and security.

How It Works

When you upload a file, our system extracts and analyzes the embedded metadata, categorizing it by risk level:

  • High Risk - Potentially sensitive information like GPS locations
  • Medium Risk - Information that could be used for profiling
  • Low Risk - Technical details with minimal privacy impact

Privacy & Ethics

This is an educational tool only. We take your privacy seriously:

  • Uploaded files are processed temporarily and then deleted
  • Only metadata is stored in our database, not file contents
  • No personal information is collected beyond what's needed for analysis
  • All analysis is performed locally on your device when possible

Read our full Disclaimer and Ethics Statement for more details.

About the Developers

This tool was created by cybersecurity students and educators to raise awareness about metadata privacy risks. Our goal is to make complex security concepts accessible to everyone.

Educational Focus

Designed specifically to help students and professionals understand metadata risks.

Open Source

The project is open source, allowing for community contributions and transparency.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly update the tool with new features and metadata detection capabilities.
